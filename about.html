Link
In this Charity, we concentrated on humanizing communal living spaces for people in distress through Art and Design. There I took a leadership role in designing and painting murals in a local School for disables children and redesigning the common living spaces of a Juvenile Detention Center. During both these projects I honed my leadership skills by leading a team of students and creating a relationship between the foundation and the student body. For this work, I was given an excellency award by Anouk Foundation at they’re annual end of the year celebratory gala.
